Common Characteristics

  • Independent posts: Reporting to Board or President
  • Externally triggered: Respond to complaints regarding project activities
  • Environmental and social mandate: All address E&S as part of mandate
  • All provide compliance oversight
  • Some provide problem-solving and advisory too
  • Common goal = to provide grievance redress and public accountability for development projects in the public and/or private sectors

Who can file complaints?

  • Inspection Panel: Two + people (including organization, association, society or other grouping)
  • CAO: 1+ individual/community in impacted area, or a duly mandated representative
  • EBRD: 1+ individuals in impacted area may seek problem solving; 1+ individuals may seek compliance review, but do not have to be directly affected parties
  • EIB: Any person or group, including civil society organizations
  • BSTDB: Any staff member, or individual or group of persons, or legal entity affected by a Bank decision, practice or operation

Redress functions

  • Inspection Panel: Compliance review
  • CAO: Compliance review, dispute resolution/problem-solving, and advisory
  • EBRD: Compliance review, problem-solving
  • EIB: Compliance review, problem-solving, and advisory; EU Ombudsman
  • BSTDB: Compliance review, problem-solving, and advisory
