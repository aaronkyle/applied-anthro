Welcome to the Office of the Compliance Advisor Ombudsman (CAO) for the International Finance Corporation (IFC) and Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency (MIGA).

This web site provides you with the information you need to lodge a complaint if you are impacted directly by an IFC or MIGA project. We also provide information on the objectives and roles of the CAO for those in the private sector, civil society, and public institutions interested in the challenges of accountability, transparency and responsiveness as we strive for more equitable and sustainable development.
